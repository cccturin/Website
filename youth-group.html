What to expect.

Youth Group at CCC is a weeknight gathering where students can hang out, play, eat, and wrestle with real questions about faith and life. We meet in our gym and common areas, and every week looks a little different — games, food, teaching from Scripture, and plenty of time to actually talk.

Our goal isn't to entertain students — it's to disciple them. We want every young person who walks in to encounter Jesus, build real friendships, and grow into the kind of faith that holds up under pressure. First-time visitors and regulars are equally welcome.
