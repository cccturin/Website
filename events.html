How it works.

Join us for CCC's 3rd Annual Community Give & Take! No money changes hands — everything is free. Bring any new or lightly-used items that you believe could be a blessing to another person in the community, and take whatever you want or need.

CCC will handle any leftover items at the end of the event, so there's no pressure to take your things back home. Just show up, share what you can, find what you need, and enjoy the morning with your neighbors.

Before you drop off

Drop-off guidelines

  • Nothing large enough to require more than one person to carry — no large furniture.
  • Nothing that would go in your garage sale "free pile" — only items that will truly be a blessing to someone else.
  • Please plan to stay a few minutes to help our volunteers organize your items into the various areas.
